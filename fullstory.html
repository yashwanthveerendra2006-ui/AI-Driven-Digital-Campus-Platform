CHARITY NSS ACTIVITY

Chairman N. Shesha Reddy Sir Donates Food and Clothes

January 2025
Aditya Institutions Campus
NSS Volunteers & Staff

Aditya Institutions Chairman Sri N. Shesha Reddy organized a meaningful charitable program aimed at supporting underprivileged communities. The initiative focused on distributing food and clothing to needy families, ensuring comfort and relief to many individuals facing difficult circumstances.

"The event was successfully conducted with the active participation of NSS volunteers and college staff members who contributed their time and effort towards community welfare."

— Aditya Institutions Administration

The donation drive highlighted the institution's commitment to social responsibility and humanity. This noble initiative represents the core values that Aditya Institutions stands for - nurturing not just academic excellence but also compassion and service to society.

Through this noble initiative, several families received essential supplies, spreading happiness and hope among the beneficiaries. The smiles on the faces of those who received help were testimony to the impact of such charitable endeavors.

Such activities continue to inspire students to actively participate in social service and nation-building programs. The event serves as a powerful reminder that education goes beyond textbooks - it encompasses building character, empathy, and a sense of responsibility towards society.

Event Highlights

  • Food packets distributed to underprivileged families
  • Clothing items provided for men, women, and children
  • Active participation from NSS volunteers
  • Support from college staff and administration
  • Multiple families benefited from the initiative

Sri N. Shesha Reddy

Chairman, Aditya Institutions

A visionary leader committed to excellence in education and community welfare.
